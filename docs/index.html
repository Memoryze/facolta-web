About us

At Facolta, we believe everyone has something they are good at or very knowledgeable about. This is even more true when a person works as an educator. However, there seems to be a gap between knowing about a thing and being able to educate another person about it.

In a bid to bridge this gap, we decided to create a free student-focused teaching curriculum with content collated from the different corners of the Web. This curriculum is a bit unconventional as it combines blog posts, podcast episodes, videos, and articles. You lean how to become a better teacher and how to make money while at it.

We also have a Slack community where educators from across the world interact, network and share life-changing tips and experiences. As an educator, you will have access to a goldmine of information with which you can take your career to the next level.
